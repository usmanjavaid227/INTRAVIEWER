Why INTRAVIEWER

Why INTRAVIEWER ?

Most job seekers are unable to give their best in interview due to anxiety and nervousness. INTRAVIEWER provide a platform to gain confidence through practice and real-time feedback.Prepare you for your next interview to get you more job offers.

First Impression

Studies show that 55% of first impressions in interviews are based on non-verbal cues like facial expressions.People have less control over facial expressions than their verbal communication

confidance

This shows how confident a candidate is based on the analysis of body movement, stress, anxiety, voice tone and posture.

Communication

This is more about the candidate's speaking style. Is the voice too loud or low? Are they speaking too fast or slow? Are they taking too many long pauses? This will help you get the details on their communication skills.

Behavior analysis

You can find more about the body movements, like eye movement, head movement and more. We can find out if a candidate is reading the answer on the screen using our AI.

Emotion intelligance

Recruiters assess Emotional Intelligence to identify the candidates with the right attitude & mindset PROCESS
